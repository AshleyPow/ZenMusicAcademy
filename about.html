About

Zen Music Academy

We are a professional music school that was established in the year 2010.Since our inception, we have successfully taught more than 3000+ students. Currently we have more than 400 students learning from us at our school alone. As a school, we have always believed in giving excellent quality of education, which can be verified by our Google reviews, testimonials of our students and more importantly, the skills on display by our students.

With more than 15 highly qualified teachers and high-quality instruments at the school, the students have an amazing time learning various instrument. Apart from lessons conducted at our centre in Mira Road and Vasai, we also conduct home tuitions all over Mumbai. We also provide lessons over Skype.

-Kevin Chetti
